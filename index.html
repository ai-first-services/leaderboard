What is AI First Services?

🔄 We are actively refining our criteria to better capture the AI-first service landscape

Definition

AI First Services are organizations where artificial intelligence is the primary foundation of their service delivery, not just a supplementary tool. These companies have built their entire business model, operations, and value proposition around AI capabilities as the core differentiator.

Listing Criteria

Revenue Range

Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) between $250K - $100M USD

Global Reach

Operating across 9 regions: North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, India, UK, Australia, Middle East, Africa, Latin America

AI-First Foundation

AI is integral to core service delivery, not an add-on feature

Service Focus

Primary business model is service delivery (not product sales)

Industry Verticals

10+ verticals including Healthcare, Finance, Education, Legal, Manufacturing, and more
